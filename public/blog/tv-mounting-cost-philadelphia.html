How Much Does TV Mounting Cost in Philadelphia?

A practical Philadelphia homeowner guide to TV mounting prices, wall types, large TVs, cord hiding, and how to avoid unsafe installs.

Typical TV mounting price ranges in Philadelphia

For a standard drywall TV mount, many Philadelphia homeowners should expect a starting range around $110 to $200 depending on TV size, wall type, bracket style, access, and whether the customer already has the mount. Larger TVs, older plaster walls, masonry walls, and cord concealment usually increase the price.

At Thomas Remodeling & Property Maintenance, simple TV mounting starts with standard pricing for under-65-inch TVs and larger 65-inch-plus TVs. The online estimator gives a starting range, then photos help confirm the final quote before the appointment.

What affects the price?

  • TV size and weight
  • Drywall, plaster, brick, or concrete wall type
  • Whether studs can be located easily
  • Mount type: fixed, tilting, full-motion, or specialty
  • Cord hiding or power relocation
  • Height and location, such as above a fireplace

Why a secure install matters

A TV mount is not just cosmetic. A poor install can damage drywall, pull out of the wall, or become a safety risk. A good install should be level, properly anchored, and placed where the customer wants it before drilling starts.

Philadelphia-specific tips

Many older Philadelphia rowhomes have plaster, uneven walls, old framing, or masonry conditions that make TV mounting less predictable than newer construction. Sending photos of the wall and mount helps avoid surprises.
